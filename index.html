What a correction actually looks like

Not just a score. The fix.

Mission: IELTS Part 2 — Describe a place you'd like to visit0:42
You
"I am wanting to visit Japan since long time because I am very interesting in their culture."
Grammar fix: "I've been wanting to visit Japan for a long time because I'm very interested in their culture." — use present perfect for ongoing desires, and "interested in" not "interesting in."
AI Coach
"Great topic. What specifically about the culture draws you in — food, history, or something else?"

Before you start

Common questions

Can AI really predict my IELTS speaking band score?

SpeakMate scores Grammar, Vocabulary, Fluency and Pronunciation after every response using the same four criteria real examiners use. Treat it as a practice estimate that shows you where to focus — not an official certified score.

Is it actually free, or is there a catch?

It's free right now with no daily limit and no account required. Pricing may change in a future version, and you'd always be notified in-app before anything requires payment.

Does SpeakMate store my voice recordings?

No. Your voice is converted to text directly on your device. The raw audio is never uploaded anywhere — only the text of what you said is used to generate feedback.

Which accent should I practice with?

Choose American, British, or Indian English depending on what your exam center or target country uses, or switch between them to build broader listening comprehension.
